Chick-fil-A Copycat Recipe

  1. Breading Ingredients
    1. All Purpose Flour,
    2. 2 tbsp. Powdered Sugar,
    3. 2 btsp. Paprika,
    4. 2 tbsp. Dried Basil (Leaves),
    5. 2 tbsp. Garlic Powder,
    6. Salt and Pepper to taste,
    7. *Add cayenne pepper if you prefer spicy*
  2. Egg Wash Ingredients
    1. 2-3 eggs,
    2. 1 cup Whole Milk,
    3. *Add a few dashes of your favorite hot sauce if you prefer spicy*
  3. Preheat Peanut Oil to 375 degrees (Great substitutes: Canola, Sunflower, Vegetable)
  4. Coat chicken breast; Alternate coats between dry breading and egg wash twice.
  5. Toast buttered brioche buns. Prepare your favorite toppings and ENJOY!!!
  6. P.S.: I prefer sweet potato fries over anything else!
